# Policies

* [POL-001](POL-001_create-policy.md) - A single policy can be created and saved  `intentional`
* [POL-002](POL-002_create-policies-rapidly.md) - Several policies created in quick succession all persist  `intentional`
* [POL-003](POL-003_policy-popup-details.md) - Opening a policy shows its details in a popup  `intentional`
* [POL-004](POL-004_manage-policy-labels.md) - Policy labels can be added, recoloured, and removed  `intentional`
* [POL-005](POL-005_no-benefit-from-cycles.md) - Benefit-from loops between layers are forbidden  `intentional`
* [POL-006](POL-006_zero-share-allowed.md) - A policy share of 0% is allowed  `intentional`
* [POL-007](POL-007_duplicate-policy.md) - Duplicating a policy copies its parameters  `intentional`
* [POL-008](POL-008_total-premium-formula.md) - Total Premium equals the sum of premium times share  `intentional`
* [POL-009](POL-009_deployed-limit-updates.md) - Deployed Limit updates when a policy changes  `intentional`
* [POL-010](POL-010_policy-dates-persist.md) - Policy dates persist after editing and duplication  `intentional`
* [POL-011](POL-011_policies-tab-baseline.md) - The Policies tab matches its accepted baseline  `characterization`
* [POL-012](POL-012_special-conditions-in-aggregation.md) - Policy special conditions are reflected in aggregation  `intentional`
* [POL-013](POL-013_delete-policy.md) - A policy can be deleted from the dustbin  `intentional`
* [POL-014](POL-014_delete-layer-updates-aggregation.md) - Deleting a layer updates the dependent aggregation  `intentional`

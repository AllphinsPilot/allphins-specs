## Objective

Prove a RAD (Risk-Attaching-During) policy considers only risks whose inception date falls within the policy dates, excluding those outside.

## Preconditions

- A portfolio with at least one LOD policy and one RAD policy exists.
- Risks exist with inception dates both inside and outside the RAD policy's date range.

## Scenario: RAD considers only in-range inception dates

```gherkin
Given a portfolio with a RAD policy
When the scenario is computed
Then only risks whose inception date is within the RAD policy dates are considered
And risks whose inception date is outside the RAD policy dates are excluded
```

## Objective

Prove the Top-Zones creation form accepts in-range Radius values and rejects out-of-range ones.

## Preconditions

- The TZ report creation form is open on the Aggregations page.

## Scenario: radius is bounded

```gherkin
Given the TZ creation form
When the Radius is set to <radius>
Then TZ creation is <result>
```

### Examples
| radius | result |
|--------|--------|
| greater than 100 km (greater than 62 mi) | not allowed |
| 100 km or less (62 mi or less) | allowed |

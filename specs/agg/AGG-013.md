## Objective
Prove the Top-Zones report creation form rejects out-of-range "Number of Top Zones" and "Radius" values and accepts in-range ones.

## Preconditions
- The TZ report creation form is open on the Aggregations page.

## Scenario: number of top zones is bounded per line of business
```gherkin
Given the TZ creation form for a "<lob>" line of business
When "Number of Top Zones" is set to <count>
Then TZ creation is <result>
```

### Examples
| lob | count | result |
|-----|-------|--------|
| Terror | 50 or more | not allowed |
| Terror | fewer than 50 | allowed |
| Property | 15 or more | not allowed |
| Property | fewer than 15 | allowed |

## Scenario: radius is bounded
```gherkin
Given the TZ creation form
When the Radius is set to <radius>
Then TZ creation is <result>
```

### Examples
| radius | result |
|--------|--------|
| greater than 100 km (greater than 62 mi) | not allowed |
| 100 km or less (62 mi or less) | allowed |
